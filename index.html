How it works

Our collection is based on face detection algorithms and reproduces what some systems see, when they look at us. The detailed patterns will be interpreted as a multitude of faces, so the algorithm won’t know which one is “real” and who it’s looking at.

Who we are

We are a fashion label in the making, inspired by and focused on the meaning of privacy within an increasingly digitalized and interconnected world. With our clothing, we aim to raise awareness for the importance of protecting one’s data and give people tools to do just that
